Design Rules (to be hardcoded in WASM preset)

  1. Text alignment: top — all lyric/group boxes use align-items: flex-start, not center
  2. Text size: maximize — auto-fit grows text as large as possible within the box, no arbitrary caps
  3. Status bar: single flex row — clock, live, connected share one bar with justify-content: space-between. No individual absolute boxes that can overlap or wrap.
  4. No wrapping anywhere — white-space: nowrap on status elements. Lyrics use pre-wrap (respect line breaks from content) but shrink font to fit width.
  5. Boxes never move — all positions are absolute percentages, independent of content
